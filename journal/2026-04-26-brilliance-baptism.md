## Sunday, April 26 — Brilliance's Baptism Day 🌟

After the service, the church showed up in a big way. Everyone was so generous — we walked away with snacks from practically everybody. That kind of community support is what makes church feel like family.

As parents, that's what we want — for our children to build relationships with people who love them.

Basketball has always been more than a sport to me — it's a way to connect, teach discipline, and build character.
