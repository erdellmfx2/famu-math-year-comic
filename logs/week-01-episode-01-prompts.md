# Image Prompts — Week 01 Episode 01

Use these prompts for concept art, panel roughs, or final image generation.

## Global Prompt Base
Modern animated comic style, clean semi-cartoon linework, expressive faces, soft shading, vibrant but balanced green orange gold palette, Florida A&M University inspired campus atmosphere, warm Florida sunlight, youth-friendly G-rated tone, polished social-media comic framing, readable composition, emotional clarity, consistent character design.

---

## Panel 1 Prompt
Malik Baptiste sitting alone at an outdoor campus table before the semester begins, Florida A&M inspired red brick campus, green trees, soft golden afternoon light, a laptop open to a budgeting spreadsheet, graph notebook and calculator on the table, students walking in the background, calm focused expression, modern animated comic style, clean lines, soft shading, vertical-friendly composition.

## Panel 2 Prompt
Over-the-shoulder view of Malik’s laptop showing a neatly organized budget spreadsheet with meal plan, textbooks, supplies, and unexpected costs, Malik leaning in with focused concentration, warm outdoor campus setting, structured academic mood, modern animated comic style, clean readable composition.

## Panel 3 Prompt
Nia Reynolds arriving beside Malik’s table with a bright amused smile, carrying a notebook and a tote bag with campus and math-club style buttons, Malik looking up with mild surprise, warm collegiate campus background, expressive character acting, modern animated comic style, semi-cartoon, soft shading.

## Panel 4 Prompt
Malik and Nia in a first-conversation two-shot at an outdoor FAMU-inspired campus table, Malik showing a tiny half-smile, Nia playful and confident, friendly academic chemistry, warm sunlight, soft background student activity, modern animated comic style, clean bold outlines, social-media comic panel framing.

---

## Negative Prompt Guidance
Avoid photorealism, extra fingers, distorted hands, cluttered backgrounds, unreadable props, text artifacts, grim tone, exaggerated parody style, or generic office-like campus scenes.

## Consistency Reminders
- Malik: deep green / charcoal / white palette, structured posture
- Nia: orange and green accents, open expressive posture
- Keep both characters college-aged and visually consistent with the character bible
