# Week 01 Episode 07 — The Edge of Beginning

## Source
- Timeline: `story/timeline-weeks/1.md`
- Prose: `story/timeline-weeks-prose/prose_1.md`

## Episode Purpose
Close Week 01 by framing FAMU's mathematics culture as rigorous but caring, and end on the unresolved tension that classes will soon test everything they have prepared.

## Episode Format
- **Recommended format:** 4-panel daily comic
- **Primary viewpoint:** closing shared reflection
- **Setting:** campus walkway at sunset, then separate late-night preparation spaces
- **Emotional arc:** professor insight -> academic belonging -> private resolve -> first-bell tension

## Panel Script

### Panel 1 — The department's message comes into focus
**Visual:** Campus walk at golden hour. Malik and Nia move side by side past a building after a department-related event.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "Anybody who says 'show your reasoning clearly' in a welcome setting is sending a message."

### Panel 2 — Malik names what he likes about it
**Visual:** Slightly closer two-shot while walking. Campus brick, banners, and warm light behind them.

**Dialogue (Malik):** "Means the department actually cares how we think, not just what we circle."

### Panel 3 — Nia chooses bravery
**Visual:** Nia alone at night closing her notebook after writing a last line. The page can show: **Be brave enough to learn in public.**

**Caption:** "She smiled nervously at how true it felt."

### Panel 4 — The first bell is close
**Visual:** Split-feeling final panel or single moody closing shot of campus at night with Malik at his desk and Nia in her room implied through framing or inset composition. The emotional emphasis is anticipation.

**Caption:** "The first bell hadn't rung yet. But it was close."
