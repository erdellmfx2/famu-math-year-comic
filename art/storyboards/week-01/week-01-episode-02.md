# Week 01 Episode 02 — A Spreadsheet Meets a Spark

## Source
- Timeline: `story/timeline-weeks/1.md`
- Prose: `story/timeline-weeks-prose/prose_1.md`

## Episode Purpose
Stage Malik and Nia's first real interaction and make their contrast immediately charming, readable, and comic-friendly.

## Episode Format
- **Recommended format:** 4-panel daily comic
- **Primary viewpoint:** shared two-hander
- **Setting:** same outdoor campus table as Episode 01
- **Emotional arc:** interruption -> curiosity -> introductions -> chemistry

## Panel Script

### Panel 1 — Nia's opening line lands
**Visual:** Over-the-shoulder shot from Malik's side as Nia appears beside the table with notebook and button-covered tote bag. She looks amused, not mean.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "That spreadsheet looks like it pays taxes for fun."

### Panel 2 — Malik recalculates
**Visual:** Medium close-up on Malik looking up, caught between confusion and dry humor.

**Dialogue (Malik):** "I'm not sure whether that's an insult."

### Panel 3 — Nia invites herself into the scene
**Visual:** Two-shot with Nia gesturing toward the empty chair, open posture, easy confidence.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "It's concern. Mind if I sit?"

### Panel 4 — Names, majors, and instant contrast
**Visual:** Seated two-shot at the table. Malik remains composed; Nia is already comfortable.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "Nia Reynolds. Mathematical Sciences."
**Dialogue (Malik):** "Malik Baptiste. Actuarial Science."
