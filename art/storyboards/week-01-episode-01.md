# Week 01 Episode 01 — Before the First Bell

## Source
- Timeline: `story/timeline-weeks/1.md`
- Prose: `story/timeline-weeks-prose/prose_1.md`

## Episode Purpose
Introduce Malik's orderly, analytical energy and establish the FAMU campus as warm, alive, and full of beginning-of-year possibility.

## Episode Format
- **Recommended format:** 4-panel daily comic
- **Primary viewpoint:** Malik-led introduction
- **Setting:** outdoor campus common-area table near walkways
- **Emotional arc:** campus anticipation -> Malik's system brain -> quiet self-pressure -> setup for interruption

## Panel Script

### Panel 1 — Campus is holding its breath
**Visual:** Warm establishing shot of FAMU in early August. Red brick buildings, sunlight, students drifting through with folders and phones, lived-in campus movement.

**Caption:** "Before the first bell, FAMU already felt like something was about to begin."

### Panel 2 — Malik builds order
**Visual:** Medium shot of Malik at an outdoor table with laptop, spreadsheet open, notebook and calculator nearby. He looks focused, structured, slightly reserved.

**Caption:** "Malik Baptiste trusted systems before he trusted surprises."

### Panel 3 — The spreadsheet reveals the stakes
**Visual:** Close shot of the laptop screen and Malik's hand adjusting numbers. Spreadsheet tabs visible: costs, meal plan, textbooks. One row labeled **Unexpected** is highlighted.

**Dialogue (Malik):** "Unexpected always gets expensive."

### Panel 4 — He thinks he can master the year
**Visual:** Reflective medium-close shot. Malik leans back, campus behind him, expression thoughtful but determined.

**Caption:** "If patterns could be learned, then maybe this year could be mastered."
