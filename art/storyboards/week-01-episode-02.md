# Week 01 Episode 02 — Nia’s system vs Malik’s system

## Source
- Timeline: `story/timeline-weeks/1.md`
- Prose: `story/timeline-weeks-prose/prose_1.md`

## Episode Purpose
Turn the first joke into an actual introduction and establish the contrast between Malik’s structured planning and Nia’s warm, playful systems thinking.

## Episode Format
- **Recommended format:** 4-panel daily comic
- **Primary viewpoint:** shared Malik/Nia two-hander
- **Setting:** same outdoor campus table as Episode 01
- **Emotional arc:** guarded reply -> playful reassurance -> easy introduction -> chemistry lands

## Character Notes for This Episode
- Malik stays measured, dry, and slightly skeptical before relaxing.
- Nia stays amused, open, and socially effortless.
- The scene should feel like the same warm afternoon only seconds after Episode 01 ends.

## Panel Script

### Panel 1 — Malik answers carefully
**Visual:** Medium shot of Malik at the outdoor table, one hand near the laptop, looking up at Nia with a guarded but curious expression.

**Dialogue (Malik):** "I’m not sure whether that’s an insult."

### Panel 2 — Nia disarms him
**Visual:** Medium close-up of Nia smiling with playful sincerity, notebook tucked under one arm and tote bag strap over her shoulder.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "It’s concern."

### Panel 3 — Invitation and introduction
**Visual:** Two-shot as Nia gestures lightly toward the empty chair and begins to settle in. Malik is a little less tense. The campus table props stay visible for continuity.

**Dialogue (Nia):** "Nia Reynolds. Mathematical Sciences."

### Panel 4 — The joke lands
**Visual:** Warm seated two-shot with both characters now in frame, conversational posture, campus life soft in the background. Nia brightens as she clocks Malik’s major while Malik looks resigned but amused.

**Dialogue (Malik):** "Malik Baptiste. Actuarial Science."
**Dialogue (Nia):** "Okay, so the spreadsheet is on brand."
