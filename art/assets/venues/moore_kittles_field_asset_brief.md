# Asset Brief — Moore–Kittles Field

## Asset Goal
Create a reusable baseball venue background for quieter sports and campus community scenes.

## Asset Type
Background plate / environment reference

## Visual Identity
- intimate college baseball environment
- smaller stands / bleachers
- dugout and backstop feel
- sunny Florida conditions
- FAMU identity through color and atmosphere

## Best Reusable Views
1. Wide field establishing view
2. First-base or third-base line view
3. Bleacher/backstop conversational angle

## Prompt Guidance
Modern animated comic style, clean semi-cartoon linework, Florida college baseball field, intimate fan environment, sunny daylight, green field, orange and green identity touches, readable background, warm community feel, not photorealistic.

## Reuse Value
Medium-high — can support:
- baseball scenes
- spring campus atmosphere
- quieter community and student-life moments

## Suggested Initial Generation Count
- 2 high-quality base images
