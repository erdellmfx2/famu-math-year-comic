# Asset Brief — Bragg Memorial Stadium

## Asset Goal
Create a reusable background asset for football-related comic scenes set at FAMU.

## Asset Type
Background plate / environment reference

## Visual Identity
- Ken Riley Field at Bragg Memorial Stadium
- large open football stadium
- collegiate field with broad seating bowl
- FAMU green and orange spirit
- warm Florida sunlight or game-day late afternoon light

## Best Reusable Views
1. Wide exterior-establishing view
2. Interior wide field view from lower stands
3. Sideline / concourse-adjacent angle usable for dialogue scenes

## Prompt Guidance
Modern animated comic style, clean semi-cartoon linework, warm Florida light, Florida A&M football stadium atmosphere, broad seating bowl, green field, HBCU game-day energy, readable composition, social-media comic background, not photorealistic, consistent with youth-friendly inspirational comic tone.

## Reuse Value
High — can support:
- football game scenes
- campus pride scenes
- recruiting moments
- character reflection shots during athletic events

## Suggested Initial Generation Count
- 2 to 3 high-quality base images
