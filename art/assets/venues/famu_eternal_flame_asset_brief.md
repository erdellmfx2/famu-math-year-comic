# Asset Brief — FAMU Eternal Flame

## Asset Goal
Create a reusable symbolic campus landmark asset centered on FAMU's Eternal Flame for reflective, inspirational, and identity-rich comic scenes.

## Reference Notes
- The Eternal Flame functions as a strong symbolic campus landmark
- Best used for moments of reflection, aspiration, tradition, legacy, and school pride
- Should feel emotionally resonant, iconic, and distinctly tied to the FAMU campus experience

## Asset Type
Background plate / landmark reference

## Visual Identity
- prominent eternal flame feature
- campus landmark setting
- reverent but welcoming tone
- strong connection to school identity, legacy, and aspiration
- works well in morning light, golden hour, or evening glow

## Best Reusable Views
1. Wide establishing shot with surrounding campus context
2. Medium landmark-focused view for dialogue/reflection scenes
3. Evening or dusk version with flame glow emphasized

## Prompt Guidance
Modern animated comic style, clean semi-cartoon linework, Florida A&M Eternal Flame campus landmark, symbolic and inspirational atmosphere, warm academic pride, readable social-media comic composition, visually iconic but grounded, not photorealistic, suitable for scenes of reflection, school pride, and personal growth.

## Reuse Value
High — can support:
- personal reflection scenes
- chapter or week transitions
- recruiting/promotional emotional moments
- legacy/tradition moments
- graduation or milestone symbolism

## Suggested Initial Generation Count
- 2 base images
