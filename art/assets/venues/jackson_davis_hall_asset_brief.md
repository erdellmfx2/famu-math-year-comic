# Asset Brief — Jackson Davis Hall (Math Building)

## Asset Goal
Create a reusable academic-building background for math-focused comic scenes.

## Reference Notes
- Also called **Jackson Davis Hall** / Jackson Davis Building
- Historic FAMU campus building
- Search references indicate construction date around **1927** and association with the **Department of Mathematics**
- Located on/near Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard on campus
- Visual relationship: historic academic building, classroom/office/meeting identity

## Asset Type
Background plate / environment reference

## Visual Identity
- historic red-brick or masonry academic building feel
- classic FAMU campus architecture
- recognizable as a math/academic home base
- approachable but serious scholarly atmosphere

## Best Reusable Views
1. Exterior establishing shot
2. Front entrance / walkway approach
3. Side angle with students entering or exiting

## Prompt Guidance
Modern animated comic style, clean semi-cartoon linework, historic Florida A&M academic building, math department home-base feeling, red-brick collegiate architecture, warm Florida sunlight, welcoming but studious atmosphere, readable for social-media comic backgrounds, not photorealistic.

## Suggested Initial Generation Count
- 2 base images
