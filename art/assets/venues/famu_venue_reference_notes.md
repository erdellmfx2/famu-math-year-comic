# FAMU Venue Reference Notes

## Bragg Memorial Stadium
- Full name: **Ken Riley Field at Bragg Memorial Stadium**
- Location: **1500 Wahnish Way, Tallahassee, Florida 32310**
- Capacity: **25,500**
- Sport use: **Football**
- Surface: **AstroTurf RootZone 3D3**
- Opened: **1957**
- Renovated: **2022**
- Visual cues to emphasize:
  - large football bowl seating
  - FAMU football atmosphere
  - green playing surface with strong college stadium scale
  - warm HBCU game-day energy

## Al Lawson Center
- Formal name: **Alfred Lawson Jr. Multipurpose Center and Teaching Gymnasium**
- Common name: **Al Lawson Center**
- Location: **1800 Wahnish Way, Tallahassee, Florida 32310**
- Capacity: **9,639 (basketball)**
- Surface: **Hardwood**
- Opened: **2009**
- Sport use: **Men's basketball, women's basketball, volleyball**
- Visual cues to emphasize:
  - indoor hardwood court
  - arena seating bowl
  - FAMU green and orange atmosphere
  - bright, modern collegiate indoor arena feel

## Moore–Kittles Field
- Home venue for: **Florida A&M baseball**
- Capacity: **500**
- Sport use: **Baseball**
- Visual cues to emphasize:
  - intimate college baseball field
  - dugout / backstop / bleacher atmosphere
  - Florida daylight and green/orange identity
  - smaller-scale, more personal fan environment than football or basketball
